— about me

My name is Jan and this is my personal website and portfolio. I'm studying Applied Informatics at the University of Economics and Business in Prague. I'm a freelance designer, photographer, videomaker and music producer. So far I've done several projects ranging from simple landing pages to entire brand identities.

You can view some of the selected projects in the sections below.

— web design

I've designed websites for several types of products. From bakeries to apparel, every site needs a different approach and as a fan of minimalistic design I believe a good website should have just the right amount of information to get the intended message across and not overwhelm the user with countless grouped menus and dropdowns.

— graphic design

Graphic design is something I am quite fond of. I love creating logos, banners, flyers etc. Every assignment is a challenge in itself starting with understanding the target audience and ending with post analysis of what worked and what didn't.
